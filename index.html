Culture of Pakistan:

Pakistani culture is enrich in traditions and represents history of this region. Pakistani people and society is unique in its way of life,thoughts and morals. The people of each province have adopted different cultural values which make them different from other groups and societies.

Our Culture:

Pakistan has a rich and unique culture that upholds traditions. Pakistani culture is rich in variety of dresses. The dress of Pakistan are very colourful and prominent and give attractive look in national fairs and festivals.

Fairs and Festivals:

The culture of Pakistan has great tradition of fairs and festivals. These fairs and festivals are held in all parts of the country. Polo festival of Gilgit is famous at national and international level.

Sports:

Pakistani people are great lovers of sports and games. Modern games like Cricket,Football,Hockey,Badminton,Squash are played throughout the country.Pakistan has produced many great players in different games.

Handicrafts:

Pakistan has made its name in handicrafts at internationnal level. Wooden furniture of Chiniot,sport goods of Sialkot and embroidery of Multan and hyderabad is famous in the world.

Language:

There are five famous Languages spoken in Pakistan.These are Punjabi,Pashto,Sindhi,Saraiki and Urdu. Urdu is the the national language of Pakistan.Punjabi is spoken much by the people.
